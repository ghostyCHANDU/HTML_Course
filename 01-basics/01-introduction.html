Just the Two of Us (Scholastic Bookshelf) by Will Smith, illustrated by Kadir Nelson The first time I saw the video for Will Smith’s song, “Just the Two of Us”, I cried. This book features the lyrics to his songs, and lovely illustrations (as usual) from Kadir Nelson..

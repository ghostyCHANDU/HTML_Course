The Evening Garden

The sun dips low behind the trees,
casting long shadows across the lawn.
A hush falls over everything,
as if the world is holding its breath.

In the garden, roses bow their heads,
their petals soft as velvet in the fading light.
The air grows cool and still.
Somewhere, a cricket begins its song.

Night approaches on gentle feet,
bringing with it the scent of jasmine,
the promise of stars,
and the quiet comfort of darkness.
